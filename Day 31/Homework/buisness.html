Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD) is an American multinational corporation

and fabless semiconductor company based in Santa Clara, California,

that designs, develops and sells computer processors and related technologies for business and consumer markets.

AMD's main products include microprocessors, motherboard chipsets, embedded processors,

graphics processors for servers, workstations, personal computers, and embedded system applications.
